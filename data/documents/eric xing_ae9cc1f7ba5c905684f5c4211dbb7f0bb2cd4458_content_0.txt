Section: G. Local CR spectral fittings
Fig. S5 shows the proton and helium spectra measured locally by balloon or space experiments [21, 22, 43, 44] and ground-based air shower experiments [45, 46]. We use the sum of two exponentially cutoff power-law functions A1(E/TeV)−B1 exp(−E/C1) + A2(E/TeV)−B2 exp(−E/C2), to fit the data. The parameters (Table S4) are adjusted to properly reflect the uncertainties of the measurements.
