Event: Lunar Bots, Roll Out!
Date: 4/11/24
Time: 3:00 PM-7:00 PM ET
Participants/Audience: Open to entire CMU community 
Event Details: 
Lunar Bots will be in the Activities Tent, which is #3 on the map.

Come check out CMU's lunar rovers, meet the students who just operated the first American robotic lunar rover in space at irisrover.space, drive one of our test robots, and get some cool space mission merch! Follow us on Instagram @iris_rover and @cmumoonranger. Additional weekend hours are 11 a.m.-7 p.m. on Friday and Saturday.

Note: No registration required. No event fee. This event is open to the CMU community.
